    Welcome!

    About Church

    Jesus Worship Ebenezer Ministry is a Christian missionary church, founded in 1998, by the man of God Bishop James M. Mucira and his wife Pastor Charity Muthike, at Kimbimbi Mwea. By the power of God and hardwork, the church has grown and ventured within and beyond parts of Kenya, aiming to reach those untouched by the Gospel.

    The mother church is located in Mwea - Kimbimbi town, while her branches include; Kathiriku church, Gathigiriri church, Nyangati church, Karira church, Ngurubani church and Nairobi church.

    Our desire is to see the worship of Jesus Christ spread across Africa and the world, through full commitment of individual lives to work of Christ.

    Our Statement of Faith

    We believe there is one God, who has revealed Himself as our Father, in His son Jesus Christ, and as the Holy Spirit. (Deuteronmy. 6:4)

    Our Mission

    To help people find their way back to God, and become the partakers of His Kingdom.

    Our Vision

    To be a global model of Evangelism that transforms our world for Jesus Christ.
